---
title: Markdown link rewriting
---

Authors often link with .md/.mdx extensions:

- [Sibling page](./sibling.mdx)
- [Up and over](../guide/intro.md)
- [With anchor](./other.mdx#install)
- [With query](./other.md?foo=bar)

External links must be left alone:

- [External](https://example.com/foo.md)
- [Mailto](mailto:hi@example.com)

Pure anchor stays untouched:

- [Anchor only](#section)
