{/*
  Admonitions are authored in JSX form here (Note/Tip/Info/Warning/Danger).
  Originally these were directive-form (`:::note[Title]`), but zfb's
  AdmonitionsPlugin is configured with `title_from_label: false`, so the
  bracketed title is dropped at parse time. Filed as an upstream enhancement
  request against Takazudo/zudo-front-builder; until that lands, JSX form is
  the supported way to attach a custom title in zudo-doc fixtures.
  See zudolab/zudo-doc#1355 (Wave 3, Topic B).
*/}
